Before you start — please read this.

This is a free, educational calculator — not financial, investment, tax, legal, or retirement advice. Using it creates no advisory or professional relationship, no fee is charged, and nothing here recommends any security or what you should do with your money.

Results are general, hypothetical illustrations from a fixed formula applied to the numbers you enter. They are not tailored to you — it's the same math for everyone, just different inputs.

The model is deliberately simplified: U.S. federal figures only (no state tax), with built-in tax, Social Security, and withdrawal numbers that can go out of date — verify them before relying on anything. The Social Security estimate is rough and tends to run high. It leaves out major risks including market crashes, the order returns arrive in, healthcare and long-term-care costs, account-type differences, and survivor situations. Real outcomes will differ, often by a lot.
